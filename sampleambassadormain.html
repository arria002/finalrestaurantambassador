TAKE OUT UPDATE!

Thank you all for the overwhelming support and business during these difficult times. We have to make a little change to help things go a little smoother for everyone. We will no longer be able to take call ahead orders for people to pick up between 4 and 7 pm everyday.

We apologize for the inconvenience, but we get a large volume of take-out orders during these times due to this high demand we can no longer guarantee call ahead orders will be ready for a specific time. We ask that you please call and place your order so we can give you a more accurate time for when your food will be ready. Thank you for your understanding and continued support!!
